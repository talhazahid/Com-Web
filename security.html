Security

Security/End-to-End Encryption Content

Your calls and messages in this chat are secured with end-to-end encryption. LEARN MORE

Comera is Safe and Secure

Comera keeps your friends and family close to you. You share photos, videos, and have all kinds of conversations. Comera is with you through difficult times too, as you stay in touch with them during natural disasters. Comera also helps you get in touch with people you hadn’t been in contact with. All this means that Comera is a part of your most personal moments, and keeping that in mind we have an end-to-end encryption function in the app which keeps your messages and everything else safe and secure.

Protection from the Start

Comera’s end-to-end encryption feature is by default activated as soon as you start using it. While several messaging apps offer this feature, Comera goes a step ahead and ensures that the conversations remain completely private. The messages or any other information cannot be accessed by anyone, and that includes Comera.

Your messages are protected by a special ‘lock’ feature that encrypts the message between you and the ones you talk to. You don’t need to follow any instructions to activate this function, as Comera offers this protection by default.

Your Calls are Secure

The calls made through Comera are secured by end-to-end encryption feature, which ensures that no third party gets access to it. Talk freely

Your Messages Belong to You

Comera doesn’t store any messages from your end, and they stay in between you and the recipient. Once the messages are delivered, the messages are erased from our servers.

Check For Yourself

You can check for yourself if end-to-end encryption is in place. Just tap on the ‘end-to-end encryption’ on top of the chat window.
