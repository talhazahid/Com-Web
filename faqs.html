Frequently Asked Questions

1.1 How do I Install it?

To install, go to https://comeratech.com/, Tap on Install Tab.

Android users can install from Google Play. https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.is.core.app .

iOS users can install from the App Store.

https://apps.apple.com/ae/app/comera/id1462023656 1.2 How do I Configure the app?

After downloading the app:

  • Tap the Open tab.
  • Read the Term and Conditions and Privacy policy carefully then Tap on Agree and Continue.
  • Select your country.
  • Enter your phone number. You will receive an OTP on your phone.
  • Enter the OTP sent to your phone
  • Allow the contacts & phone call permissions
  • Setup your profile with profile picture and profile name to start using it.
1.3 How do I update Comera?

Comera is automatically updated.

To update manually:

Android users can go to Google Play.

https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.is.core.app

iOS users can update from the App Store.

https://apps.apple.com/ae/app/comera/id1462023656 1.4 How do I verify my number?

Comera verifies your number through OTP (One-time Password) at the time of registering for the app or when you change the number.

1.5 How do I request a new OTP?

In case you entered the wrong number:

  • Edit the number and Tap on ‘Next’ to receive a new OTP. You shall now receive an SMS for authentication.

In case you made a delay in entering the OTP, or the OTP didn’t deliver at all:

  • Tap on ‘Resend’ to receive a new OTP. You will now receive an SMS for authentication.

2.1 How do I sync my contacts?

Comera syncs the contacts from your phonebook automatically.

2.2 How do I add a contact?
  • Go to the phonebook of your device.
  • Add the contact there.
  • The new contact will directly sync in with your app’s contact list.
2.3 How do I edit/ update my contacts?
  • Go to the phonebook of your device
  • Select the contact you desire to update
  • Edit it and Save
  • The change will automatically sync in with your app’s contact list
2.4 Can I delete a contact from Comera

You cannot delete a contact directly from Comera. If you desire to delete a contact, go to your phonebook and delete the added contact from there. Your action in the phonebook will delete the same contact from Comera’s contact list as well.

2.5 How do I block a contact?

There are two options for the user to block a contact:

Blocking a Contact from Privacy Settings
  • Go to App Settings
  • Select ‘Privacy’
  • Tap on ‘Blocked Contact’
  • Tap on the option to add a new contact in the block list on the top right part of the screen
  • Select the contact you want to block
Blocking a Contact from Chats
  • Go to chat.
  • Select the contact.
  • Go to settings.
  • Choose the ‘View Contact’ option.
  • Tap on the block option.
2.6 How do I invite a friend?

3.1 How can I make an audio call?

A user can place the audio call to contact when they are on the contacts screen, chat screen or the call logs screen.

Audio Call from the Contact Screen:
  • Open the App’s contact screen.
  • Select a contact from the contact list you wish to call
  • Tap on the handset icon to place the audio call
Audio Call from the Chat Screen:
  • Go to ‘Contacts’
  • Tap on the contact you wish to call
  • A chatbox will open with the contact
  • Tap on the handset icon to place the audio call
Audio Call from the Call Log Screen:
  • Go to the App’s Call Log screen.
  • Tap on the contact you want to place the call to*
  • Tap on the handset icon to place the audio call
*The desired contact will only appear on this screen if you called them recently 3.2 How can I make a video call?

Video Call from the Chat Screen:

Video Call from the Call Log Screen:

  • Tap on the video call icon (Camcorder) to place the video call
*The desired contact will only appear on this screen if you called them recently 3.3 How do I put a call on hold?

You can put a call on hold only when there is an incoming GSM call or a call from another VoIP app. When there is one such incoming call, you will have the following options to choose from:

  • Hold and Accept: To put Comera call on hold and accept the incoming GSM/VoIP call (Call will automatically unhold once the GSM or VoIP app call ends)
  • End and Accept: To End Comera call and accept the incoming GSM/VoIP call
  • Decline: To reject the incoming GSM/VoIP call and continue with the on-going Comera call
3.4 How do I check my call history?
  • Go to the ‘Call’ screen on the app.
  • Your entire call history will be visible.
  • You can tap on a particular contact to view the call details like time, date, and connectivity status (missed, incoming, outgoing).
3.5 How do I delete a calling thread?
  • Tap on calls.
  • Select call thread.
  • Press and hold.
  • Choose the delete option from the pop-up.

OR

  • Swipe left on a call log.
  • Tap on the delete option.
3.6 In which countries is the call feature unavailable?

You can call anywhere in the world you want where people can download Comera to receive your calls on it.

3.7 Does Comera have conference call feature?

Currently, the conference call feature does not exist on Comera. The feature, however, will soon be available for which you will receive a due notification, and which you will be able to access after updating your app.

4.27 How do I check my message status?

You can determine the message status through the status of tick marks or the symbol beside your message and in the ‘Info’ section of the message; details of the different statuses are below:

  • Clock: Shows pending/waiting status and means that the message isn’t sent yet due to poor or no internet connectivity
  • Single Tick: You have sent the message, but the receiver has not yet received it
  • Double Tick: The receiver has received the message
  • Double Ticks in Green: The receiver has seen the message

The app logs a user out of their account only when they set their Comera account up on another device. The action ensures complete security and protection of the user, their account, and data.
