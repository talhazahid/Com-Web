Comera - Privacy Policy

When you trust Comera for your free calling and messaging needs, you share certain information about you with us. We remain committed to the privacy and protection of all that data and therefore, explain here in transparent terms how we do so.

By using Comera, which allows you to communicate through calls and messages over the internet, along with sharing of multimedia/files, (collectively also referred in the document as ‘We’, ‘Services’, ‘The App’), you (‘User’) acknowledge and agree to the terms and policies stated in this document. You also agree that these policies apply to all the information you provide us periodically throughout your usage of the app. The stated policies will also govern any information collected either through your doing or through the initiatives of third-party.

Storage of Information

To set up an account over Comera and to use that account, the app would ask for certain permissions from the user. Some of these requests will pop-up during the registration process, while others will appear during your usage of the app. Only when the user approves these requests, does the app perform the desired action and handle the relevant user data. The specific details of this are below:

Account Details

These are primarily the credentials you feed the app to register an account. Typically, this includes nothing more than the following:

  • Your name
  • Operational phone number
  • Profile picture (Optional)

Besides this, any other information that you may enter at this stage to set up your profile will also get logged into the system.

Messages

Your messages, which our end-to-end encryption services secure by encrypting them (including chats, calls, and multimedia content like photos, videos, and voice messages), remain in our system up to the point of delivery, which is a maximum of 7 days. Your device, however, may independently accumulate sent and received messages. To know more about how your chat is made secure, read the section on ‘Security Protocols’.

Call Information

When you place calls to your contacts through Comera, the app generates a call log where you can view the information regarding all the calls. The call log will show you the entire call history including the date and timestamps. Essentially, this is to help you instantly connect with those who you have recently been in contact with or the people you love to talk to the most to overall improve your Comera experience.

Connection Information

Information of technical nature, including details of your device of use such as hardware model, device ID, and Operating System (OS), along with IP Address, the network used to install Comera, location of the device (if you enable app’s location feature for any purpose), log files, crashes, and reports on performance, are registered in our system to keep the app technically viable at all times. In case you report a certain issue with your account, this information helps us diagnose and troubleshoot the problem more efficiently. It also helps us prepare our systems in advance to deal with potential bugs and glitches.

Status Details

Whenever you update your status information, it automatically updates in our system as well, including your online/offline status and your recent usage of our services (last seen).

Your Communication with Comera

All your correspondence with Comera including feedback, suggestions, bug reports, reporting of objectionable content, or any conversations conducted either via the app or through other means of contact including emails or website contact form, are kept saved for recordkeeping and continuous improvement purposes.

Information Sharing with a Third-Party

During your usage of the app, some third parties may also provide us with certain information about you. Typically, this includes your friends on Comera, who automatically deliver your phone number(s) when they contact you. Similarly, any profile detail, including your name, phone number, or any other personal detail made public by you will be storable or usable by other Comera users. Any message sent to other users using the service will also be available to them for use in whichever way they like. It is, therefore, your responsibility to not disclose people anything that may be sensitive to you in nature.

Similarly, when you involve a third-party service on Comera, say to share some of your Comera activities over their platform, we may receive some of the data those services may have of you. Lastly, for our marketing and commercial requirements, a part of the profiling done on the user may be shared with a third-party vendor to optimize the resources invested. Please note that the privacy policies and terms of such third-parties govern the usage of shared information, and not of Comera’s.

Third-Party Services: Any third-party services (Google Drive or iCloud), that you use and are, in any way, linked with our services, will lead to information shared with said third-party. Keep in mind, the privacy policies and terms of use of third-party services come into effect when you use their services.

How We Use Information

We store the listed information to improve, customize, and optimize our services.

Our Services: The stored information helps us to understand the usage of our services; we assess and upgrade our services as per your requirements. Furthermore, we use your information to reply to your queries.

Safety and Security: To make sure that users do not violate our Terms of services, including the usage of our service for any illegal purpose, all accounts, and their activities are verified.

No Third-Party Banners: Our services do not allow third-party banners/ads in the app.

Commercial Messaging: We will permit businesses to communicate with you for commercial purposes such as appointment information, transactions, product order, and delivery notifications, etc.

Additionally, we use the information for the following purposes:

  • To verify and authenticate your account to make your presence and experience safe and secure.
  • To connect you with your friends and family.
  • To analyze your preferences and personalize your account accordingly.
  • To report your updates and changes made periodically in the app or any part associated with it.
  • To make the experience free of suspicious or illicit activities.
  • To market our services and widen the Comera community.
  • To remove technical errors and improve the overall experience.
  • To provide you with customer support and effectively solve the issues arising from your usage of the app.
Your Control on Information

As a Comera user, you are free to control, alter, delete, or primarily; manage your information, whenever you want. More details regarding your account information:

Information Accessibility: Your information such as, profile name, photo, online status, status message, recently viewed items, or phone number may be accessible to someone who uses our services. You can go to service settings to manage who can see, store, or share your personal information.

Third-Party Providers: To upgrade and to better market our services, we work with third-party providers. Whenever the need arises to share information, we will always ask permission from you or move according to the agreed Term of Services.

Account Deletion: If you choose to delete your account, your messages too will be erased, including the undelivered ones. We will also delete any other data that is not necessitated by law for retention. Even the messages that remain undelivered will erase from our systems after a period of maximum 7 days.

Information Management

Since Comera deals extensively with the exchange of information, we understand how imperative it is to safeguard that information. For that, we have placed the best security protocols in the structure of the app, including end-to-end encryption for your messages. End-to-end encryption means that nobody, except the generator of the message and the intended receiver, will have the access to your private conversations. The protocol also covers one-on-one and conference calls so that no unintended third-party can eavesdrop.

Security Protocols

Settings: At any time, you can change the information available to others. You can use the block feature to prevent certain users from making contact.

Switch to a New Number: You can easily transition from your old phone number to a new one with the in-app number feature; this will allow you to transfer your account and the related information to a new number with ease.

Children Under 13

Comera neither permits nor encourages the use of the app by children under the age of 13. If you as a parent are setting up an account for such minors, you must do so under your credentials and supervision. In essence, Comera does not intentionally store any personal information of any minor.
